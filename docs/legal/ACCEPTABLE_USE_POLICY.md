# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Last Updated:** October 28, 2025  
**Effective Date:** October 28, 2025

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of the Encypher Enterprise API (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to comply with this AUP.

Violation of this AUP may result in suspension or termination of your account, as described in our Terms of Service.

## 1. Permitted Uses

You may use the Service to:

### 1.1 Content Authentication
- Sign authentic content you own or have rights to publish
- Verify the authenticity and integrity of signed content
- Track the provenance of your published content
- Generate cryptographic proofs for content attribution

### 1.2 Legal and Compliance
- Create evidence for legal proceedings
- Comply with regulatory requirements
- Maintain audit trails for compliance purposes
- Protect intellectual property rights

### 1.3 Anti-Plagiarism
- Detect unauthorized use of your content
- Generate attribution reports
- Identify content theft
- Protect original works

### 1.4 Professional Use
- Sign articles, legal documents, contracts, and reports
- Authenticate AI-generated content
- Verify research papers and academic works
- Protect corporate communications

## 2. Prohibited Uses

### 2.1 Fraudulent Activity

You may NOT use the Service to:

- **Sign False Content**: Create signatures for content you know to be false, misleading, or fraudulent
- **Impersonation**: Impersonate other publishers, organizations, or individuals
- **Fake Attribution**: Falsely attribute content to authors who did not create it
- **Backdating**: Manipulate timestamps to misrepresent when content was created
- **Certificate Fraud**: Obtain or use certificates under false pretenses

### 2.2 Intellectual Property Violations

You may NOT use the Service to:

- **Sign Infringing Content**: Sign content that infringes copyright, trademark, or other intellectual property rights
- **Unauthorized Content**: Sign content you do not own or have rights to
- **Plagiarized Content**: Sign content copied from others without permission
- **Stolen Content**: Sign content obtained through unauthorized means

### 2.3 Illegal Content

You may NOT use the Service to sign:

- **Defamatory Content**: Content that defames, libels, or slanders individuals or organizations
- **Obscene Content**: Pornographic, obscene, or sexually explicit material
- **Hate Speech**: Content that promotes hatred, violence, or discrimination based on race, religion, ethnicity, national origin, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
- **Terrorist Content**: Content that promotes, encourages, or facilitates terrorism or violent extremism
- **Child Exploitation**: Any content involving the exploitation or abuse of children
- **Illegal Activities**: Content that promotes or facilitates illegal activities

### 2.4 Harmful Content

You may NOT use the Service to sign:

- **Malware**: Content containing viruses, malware, or malicious code
- **Phishing**: Content designed to deceive users or steal credentials
- **Scams**: Content promoting fraudulent schemes or scams
- **Misinformation**: Content deliberately designed to spread false information (e.g., health misinformation during public health crises)

### 2.5 Abuse of Service

You may NOT:

- **Circumvent Rate Limits**: Use multiple accounts, API keys, or technical means to bypass rate limits or quotas
- **Automated Abuse**: Use bots, scripts, or automated tools to abuse the Service
- **Reverse Engineering**: Attempt to reverse engineer, decompile, or extract cryptographic keys from the Service
- **Security Attacks**: Attempt to breach, hack, or compromise the security of the Service
- **DDoS Attacks**: Launch denial-of-service or distributed denial-of-service attacks
- **Vulnerability Exploitation**: Exploit security vulnerabilities without responsible disclosure
- **Unauthorized Access**: Access accounts, data, or systems you are not authorized to access

### 2.6 Resale and Redistribution

You may NOT:

- **Resell Service**: Resell, sublicense, or redistribute the Service without written authorization
- **White-Label Abuse**: Use the Service to create competing products without authorization
- **API Key Sharing**: Share your API keys with unauthorized third parties
- **Credential Sharing**: Share account credentials with others

### 2.7 Spam and Bulk Abuse

You may NOT:

- **Spam**: Use the Service to send unsolicited bulk communications
- **Content Farming**: Sign large volumes of low-quality or duplicate content
- **SEO Manipulation**: Use signatures to manipulate search engine rankings
- **Fake Reviews**: Sign fake reviews, testimonials, or endorsements

You are solely responsible for:
- All content you sign using the Service

- Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

Encypher:
- Does not review, approve, or endorse content you sign
- Is not responsible for the accuracy or legality of your content
- Does not guarantee that signed content is truthful or authentic
- Acts solely as a technical service provider

### 3.3 Content Removal

We reserve the right to:

- Suspend or terminate accounts that repeatedly violate this AUP
- Report illegal content to law enforcement

## 4. API Usage Guidelines

### 4.1 Rate Limits

You must:
- Respect rate limits for your service tier
- Implement exponential backoff for failed requests
- Cache verification results when appropriate
- Avoid unnecessary API calls

### 4.2 API Key Security

You must:
- Keep API keys confidential and secure
- Not share API keys with unauthorized parties
- Rotate API keys regularly
- Report compromised keys immediately to legal@encypherai.com

### 4.3 Error Handling

You must:
- Handle API errors gracefully
- Not retry failed requests excessively
- Log errors for debugging purposes
- Contact support for persistent errors

## 5. Security Requirements

### 5.1 Account Security

You must:
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable multi-factor authentication (when available)
- Monitor account activity for unauthorized access
- Report security incidents immediately

### 5.2 Data Protection

You must:
- Protect Personal Data in accordance with applicable laws

### 5.3 Responsible Disclosure

If you discover a security vulnerability:

If we suspect a violation of this AUP, we may:

- Request information from you
- Temporarily suspend your account pending investigation
- Review your usage logs and signed content

### 7.2 Enforcement Actions

Depending on the severity and frequency of violations, we may:

**First Violation (Minor):**
- Issue a warning
- Require corrective action
- Provide guidance on compliance

**Repeat Violations (Moderate):**

- Require additional verification

**Severe Violations:**
- Permanently terminate account
