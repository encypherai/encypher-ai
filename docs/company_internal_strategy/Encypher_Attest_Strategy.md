## Enterprise AI Content Authenticity and Governance

**Last Updated:** March 21, 2026
**Status:** Active Product Strategy
**Version:** 1.0
**Distribution:** Executive, Product, Engineering, Sales

---

## Executive Summary

Encypher Attest is Encypher's second product line, focused on enterprise AI-generated content authenticity, governance, and evidentiary workflows. While the publisher coalition motion drives market-scale provenance infrastructure, Attest captures enterprise demand in legal, financial services, healthcare, and regulated content workflows.

Attest's core value: convert unverified AI-assisted output into cryptographically attested, policy-governed records suitable for internal controls and external scrutiny.

---

## Product Vision

Enable organizations to prove:
1. what AI-assisted content was generated,
2. what changed during human review,
3. who approved it,
4. and whether final output is authentic and untampered.

This is not only compliance tooling. It is trust infrastructure for high-stakes AI workflows.

Attest extends beyond text documents to cover all media types produced in AI-assisted workflows: images generated by DALL-E/Midjourney/Stable Diffusion, audio from text-to-speech systems, and video from AI generation tools. The same attestation workflow applies: capture original AI output, track human review changes, record approval, verify authenticity.

## Packaging and Pricing Direction

Attest should be sold as enterprise software with:
- base platform fee,
- usage/workflow volume components,
- optional advanced controls and evidence exports.

Avoid positioning Attest as a one-off feature add-on. It should be a distinct product track with a clear enterprise ACV narrative.

---

## Data Custody and Trust Requirements

Attest introduces elevated data custody responsibilities when storing source/original text.

Minimum strategy requirements:
- explicit retention windows,
- deletion/right-to-erasure workflows,
- customer-visible data practices,
- enterprise controls for reduced custody modes (e.g., hash-only behavior where feasible),
- legal alignment (DPA posture for regulated buyers).

## Risks and Mitigations

### Risk: Product ambiguity vs. core publisher motion
**Mitigation:** treat Attest as a clearly named second product line with separate ICP messaging and KPIs.

### Risk: Data custody concerns slow enterprise adoption
**Mitigation:** prioritize retention/deletion controls and customer-facing data practices documentation.

### Risk: Feature spread without clear wedge
**Mitigation:** focus on high-value attestation workflows first, then expand.

## Conclusion

Encypher Attest gives Encypher a high-value enterprise lane adjacent to publisher licensing infrastructure. It translates standards authority into workflow trust, governance, and evidentiary outcomes that enterprises will pay for now, independent of coalition timeline.
