## Notes on Tone and Approach

**Pattern across all messages:**
- Lead with what's happening (SC application) -- no buildup
- State exactly what you want to say about them -- let them approve or adjust
- Offer them control over the characterization
- Don't ask anyone to write anything (except River Valley, optional)
- Keep every message under 150 words of body text
- No marketing language, no pitch, no sell

**Messages 1-8** are fact confirmations from existing relationships. Keep them
operational and brief.

**Message 9** (Jeremy Uzan) is a warmer introduction that requests a conversation
first, then a potential reference. Two-step: call first, then confirm what you
can say.

**Message 10** (River Valley) is the only one where a brief written statement
is appropriate, because they're a fellow C2PA member and peer validation from
inside the community carries a specific kind of weight.
