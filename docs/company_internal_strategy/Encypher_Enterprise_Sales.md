## Core Sales Narrative

### The Market Reality

Text on the open web has no cryptographic proof of origin. When content is copy-pasted, scraped, or used to train AI models, ownership connections disappear. Every enterprise deploying AI at scale faces this reality: your content exists without mathematical proof it's yours.

As Co-Chair of the C2PA Text Provenance Task Force, working with Google, BBC, OpenAI, Adobe, and Microsoft, we're establishing text authentication as the global standard. Every enterprise faces a choice: implement basic compliance or deploy our enhanced solution with patent-pending capabilities that provide governance, intelligence, and proof.

The EU AI Act demands verifiable authenticity. Your competitors are gaining attribution intelligence. The market is converging on standards-based text provenance infrastructure.

### Encypher Enterprise: The Complete Solution

**Standards Foundation—Baseline**
We co-chair the C2PA Text Provenance Task Force. Our implementation ensures compatibility with the emerging global standard while preparing you for regulatory requirements.

**Enterprise Enhancements—Differentiation**
Our patent-pending technology goes beyond basic C2PA:
- **Cryptographic Watermarking:** Invisible to readers, embedded in text—survives copy-paste and web scraping
- **Sentence-Level Granularity:** Track and authenticate content at sentence, paragraph, or document level
- **Multi-Layer Hashing:** Detect tampering at precise locations within content
- **Formal Notice Capability:** Serve cryptographically-backed notice that content is marked and owned
- **Directory-Scale Operations:** Sign and verify entire content repositories
- **Performance Intelligence:** Real-time dashboards showing content attribution and model optimization insights
