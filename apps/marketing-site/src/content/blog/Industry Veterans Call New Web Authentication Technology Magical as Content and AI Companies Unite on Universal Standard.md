### 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠅄󠄃󠄺󠆔󠇇󠇏󠄺󠄯󠆡󠆷󠆅󠄅󠅪󠇪󠆊󠆓︄󠅨󠇪󠄓󠆷󠇦󠆲󠅞󠅹󠆡󠆝︀󠇘󠇋󠅩󠆲󠆱󠆄󠄘󠇨󠅛󠄽󠅧󠅚Encypher Delivers Breakthrough Solution to C2PA's Highest-Priority Challenge, Enabling Verifiable Web Content Authenticity

**Portland, OR – July 21st, 2025** 

Leading technologists at major content and AI companies are calling new text authentication technology 'magical' after witnessing demonstrations of what was previously considered impossible. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠇤󠄫󠆨󠇪󠅰󠆉󠄲󠇔󠆈󠅎󠆋󠅦󠇡󠇪󠇍󠆃󠆸󠆶󠄲󠇃︂󠆆󠆭󠄿󠄈󠇟󠅑󠇍󠆇󠇍󠆁󠇑󠇈󠅚󠅑󠅤󠄯󠄿󠆃󠆀Encypher, collaborating with Coalition for Content Provenance and Authenticity consortium and BBC & Adobe researchers, has solved the technical challenge that stumped the industry for years - embedding cryptographic proof into web text that's completely invisible yet mathematically verifiable. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠅴󠅐󠅦󠄆󠇪󠅀󠄳󠄫󠆟󠇢󠆦󠇣󠄱󠅔󠄀󠆽󠇫󠅲󠄆󠅞󠅉󠅀󠅳󠄝󠄆󠆜󠆅󠄒󠅋󠆝󠄪󠆎󠄇󠅞󠆙󠇦󠆰󠆭󠅂󠄶The breakthrough enables content publishers to protect intellectual property while allowing AI developers to build ethical, transparent training datasets. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠄏󠆕󠇖󠅿󠆊󠅢󠄴󠅀󠆦󠄧󠆡󠆌󠅂󠅺󠇛󠅷󠄼󠆂󠆎󠅌󠆒󠅲󠄤󠆿󠅠󠆚󠅕󠇦󠅨󠅮󠇧󠇖󠄷︌󠇕󠄷︎󠆴󠄹󠆴The innovation directly addresses what C2PA consortium members identified as their "#1 technical challenge for text" - creating tamper-evident metadata for digital content that maintains readability while providing court-admissible proof of origin. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠆛󠇡󠇂󠄆󠆢︂󠄿󠆄󠆪󠆹󠆳󠄳︍󠆘󠆹󠄫︋󠄽󠅇󠆂󠆽󠆁󠅽󠅄󠅚󠄴󠄲󠄘󠇮󠇎︊󠆐󠄶󠇆󠅭󠆏󠄇󠄲︊󠆤Unlike statistical AI detection tools that achieve accuracy rates equivalent to coin-flip probability, Encypher's cryptographic approach provides mathematical certainty that courts and regulators demand. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠇪󠆄󠇇󠄨󠆀󠅻󠄱󠆘󠆅󠄞󠅥󠅍󠅶󠇀󠄭󠇬󠆺󠅌󠇪󠆓󠄣󠇕󠅃󠅘󠆐󠆯󠇮󠇌󠄃︋󠅀󠆥󠅺󠅾󠇨󠇊󠅻󠅎󠄪󠅌C2PA consortium members have described the technology as "magical" after witnessing demonstrations of invisible cryptographic signatures embedded into text without affecting readability or performance. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠇚󠄾︆󠇔󠆍󠅠󠄺󠅪󠅳󠄽󠅱󠇐󠆾︍󠇥︎󠆑󠄕󠇛󠇨︀󠆳󠇚󠇈󠅅󠅓󠇪󠄾︌󠄿︉󠄩󠅠󠆐󠄬󠄘󠆜󠅭󠄍󠆃The capability to embed tamper-evident metadata into any digital text - from news articles to blog posts - while maintaining complete invisibility represents a breakthrough that industry technologists previously considered impossible.

## Transforming Industry Collaboration

The technology creates a foundation for unprecedented collaboration between content creators and AI developers. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠅞︈󠇨󠅆󠆧󠇁󠄻󠄃󠆤󠅅󠆵󠆞󠆩󠄮󠆴󠆤󠅬󠇢󠆯󠇍󠇁󠆪󠆣󠅿󠆮󠇑󠆚󠆰󠆾󠄓󠆚󠇡󠇇︂󠄤󠆔󠇭󠇖󠅀󠆭Publishers can assert verifiable ownership of their intellectual property while AI companies gain access to transparent, licensable training data with cryptographic provenance guarantees. "󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠆺󠆨󠇦󠆅󠄬󠅤󠄼󠇀󠆀󠇎️󠇩󠅶󠆦󠇘󠇬󠅐󠅑󠄎󠅊󠇈󠅠󠇮󠄼󠄔󠄎󠆛󠆳󠄕󠆕󠆿󠄹󠆹󠄺󠇑󠆵󠆣󠇫󠆳󠅮

## Addressing the Billion-Dollar Evidence Crisis

The announcement comes as high-profile AI copyright litigation - including The New York Times vs. OpenAI and Universal Music Group vs. Anthropic - struggles with fundamental evidence challenges. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠆖󠆸󠄧󠆢󠅺︋󠄰󠆷󠆮󠇬󠇚󠅺󠄏󠆄󠅟󠅑󠆽󠇞󠅌󠅬󠄀󠇀󠄺󠆊󠆰󠄰󠅷󠄤󠄩󠆳󠆔󠄥󠆈󠇃󠆖󠅍󠆉󠄘󠅲󠅲Courts increasingly reject unreliable statistical AI detection tools, creating what legal experts call an "evidence crisis" in billion-dollar copyright cases. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠄹󠄛󠅽󠄁󠆫󠆘󠄷󠇗󠆢󠇡󠆎︍󠅺️󠄵󠆰󠇁󠅇󠆯󠇭󠇯󠇈󠆪󠄈󠅕󠇅󠄛󠆐󠅈󠅠󠅃󠅾󠇡󠅉󠄭󠇚󠇈󠆚󠇘󠇧Encypher's Unicode-based approach embeds Digital Signature-verified metadata directly into text using non-printing variation selectors, creating tamper-evident proof that survives copying, reformatting, and distribution across platforms. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠄮󠄝󠄂󠄄︂󠆆󠄳󠅽󠆝󠅖󠇗󠆫󠇆󠅎󠄳󠅇󠄅󠇩󠇑󠅽︇󠆗󠄁󠆊󠄠󠄑︋󠅡󠇭󠇉󠄕︎󠇋󠅬󠆹󠄋󠇑󠇟󠆰󠄠The technology provides the cryptographic certainty that legal professionals and regulators require for enforcement and compliance.

"󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠅬󠆙󠆁󠆯󠆆󠆹󠄽󠇃󠅽󠄺󠇁󠅬︂󠇞󠄅󠆕󠇕󠅽󠆟󠇚󠅹󠇆󠅹󠄔󠅇󠆱󠇘󠆯󠇝󠄙︎󠆷󠇕󠅶󠆠󠅛󠅹󠅹︇󠆐

## Regulatory Compliance Ready

The technology directly addresses emerging regulatory requirements, including the EU AI Act's mandatory content disclosure requirements effective August 2025, with potential fines up to €35 million for non-compliance. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠅴︁󠆼󠄽󠅗󠄘󠄵󠇈󠆏󠄉󠆄󠆂󠆎󠅱󠇞󠆣󠆓󠄢󠆖󠅜󠇊󠅋󠄷󠄑󠄜󠇒󠅡󠄆󠄎︄󠅆︉󠄑󠅱󠅨󠄖︈󠇤︂󠄴California's SB 942 similarly requires AI content identification capabilities for companies serving California users starting January 2026. "󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠄯󠄖󠆓󠅌󠇉󠅴󠄰󠅯󠆐󠇕󠇑󠅇󠇥󠆳︈󠆋󠅧󠆛󠄫󠄟󠇃󠇇󠇠󠅑󠆢󠄘󠆛󠇙󠅈︎󠇖󠇓󠆄󠄻󠄤󠅔󠆥󠄛󠅱󠆄Regulatory pressure is creating urgent demand for verifiable AI content authenticity," explains Svilich. "󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢︈󠇣︊󠅃󠇙󠅩󠄺󠄓󠆞󠅺󠅻󠇢󠄤󠇒󠅺󠆶︁󠆡󠅆󠄑󠅰󠄱󠆎󠄔󠆔󠇖󠅄󠇘󠆞󠇍󠄚󠄭󠅏󠆚󠄪󠇃󠄇󠅿︂󠄬Our solution provides the mathematical certainty that regulators and courts demand while enabling the transparency that builds public trust in AI systems. "󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠅾󠆹󠄰󠆼󠆑󠇪󠄼󠇍󠆗󠆿󠆣󠄾󠅐󠄩󠇩󠅣󠅅󠅀󠅀󠇀︂󠇋󠆍󠇦󠅉󠇌︊󠆬󠇈󠇣󠇆󠇈󠆍󠅜󠇑󠇆󠆬󠄭󠄢󠄃

## Technical Innovation

The breakthrough leverages Unicode variation selectors to create an invisible "carrier wave" for cryptographic metadata that doesn't affect text rendering or performance. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠄭󠄮󠇁󠇝󠇞󠅺󠄿󠆼󠆩󠅸󠇎󠇔󠅪󠄳󠆢󠆛󠅠󠆪󠄃󠅏󠇆󠄧󠇈󠄙󠄃󠆶󠅜󠅴󠄖󠄴󠆜󠆓󠅩󠄌󠄦󠄱️󠇫󠅞󠆭The approach has undergone extensive validation with Unicode experts at Adobe to ensure compatibility with evolving Unicode standards. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠇤󠆁󠅱󠅣󠇌󠄍󠄵󠄉󠆤󠄣󠆠󠆹︋󠄤󠇪󠅝󠇒󠇭󠅦︋︅󠄸󠆃󠄞󠅱󠇏󠆸󠇊󠆑󠇣󠅽󠄴󠆉︀󠆠󠄭︀󠅆󠄕󠅮Key technical capabilities include:
- **Cryptographic Certainty**: Digital signature verification eliminates false positives
- **Universal Compatibility**: Works with any text format, platform, or rendering system  
- **Invisible Integration**: Zero visual impact on content presentation or user experience
- **Tamper Evidence**: Modifications break cryptographic verification, detecting unauthorized changes
- **Scalable Implementation**: Lightweight integration requiring minimal computational overhead
