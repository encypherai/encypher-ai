**By: Erik Svilich, Founder & CEO | Encypher | C2PA Text Co-Chair**

For the last two years, the content industry has been at a crime scene. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠅢󠇥󠄼󠆻󠇦󠅿󠄲󠆈󠆡󠄴󠅑󠇣󠇕󠅍︂󠅆󠇃󠆸󠅌󠅫󠄺󠅰󠇅󠅠󠄇󠆏︄󠅷󠆩󠇚󠅴󠄼󠅖󠅼󠇘󠅁󠇣󠅕︋󠄕We see the impact, we feel the loss, but the trail goes cold. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢︊󠆟󠅐󠄵󠅞︇󠄳󠅮󠆊󠇍󠄎󠇋󠄾󠇑󠄲󠆇󠇯󠄴󠆺󠆿󠄶󠄂︌󠄿󠄽󠇠︁︈️󠅤󠄗󠅪󠄙󠅞󠆴󠆵󠇭︊󠅙󠆿We rely on statistical “AI detectors” to find proof, which is the equivalent of dusting for fingerprints when the culprit wore gloves. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠄭︆󠆈󠄁󠇗󠇌󠄵󠄈󠅱󠅌󠅊󠅋︄󠅟󠅑󠄥󠇨󠇬󠅖󠆀󠆫󠄃︍󠄘󠆨󠆶󠇉󠅕󠄧󠆍󠇚󠄬󠇅󠆚󠄐󠅞︂󠅦󠅌󠇟The result is a 26% accuracy rate, making every legal action a high-risk gamble. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠆏󠅑󠇯󠅸󠄋󠆋󠄹󠅝󠅶󠆙󠇕󠆰󠇔󠅮󠇅󠇔󠆩󠄧󠆤󠇦󠄾󠇯󠅵︉󠆕󠇋󠅕󠄞󠇥󠇬󠆓󠇘󠇁󠆘︅󠄠󠄎󠄳󠅒󠅩The recent $1.5B Anthropic-publisher settlement only proves the stakes. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠆳󠄙󠆕󠅔󠇢󠅱󠄴󠅴󠆩󠆮󠇚󠄲󠆕󠇕󠅨󠄌󠇢︁󠅋󠇗󠄚󠇪󠄮󠇘󠄈󠅣󠄾󠆲󠅀︀󠄁󠅼󠆇󠆺󠄿󠆀󠇡󠄂󠆃󠅲This evidence gap has locked us in a defensive battle, forcing us to ask: "How do we stop AI from using our work? "󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠄗󠅅󠄔󠇎󠆇󠅝󠄾󠆵󠆑󠇩󠆡󠄭󠆹󠆻󠅙󠅰︍󠇩󠄪󠄮󠇃󠆀󠄒󠅗󠆜󠇫󠇐󠇦󠄸󠅎󠄐︉󠆐󠅴󠆞󠇎󠅄󠆩󠇧︀

It's the wrong question. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠆎󠄳󠄙󠄶󠄪󠇪󠄱󠅜󠆔󠆦󠇍󠄲󠄾󠇪󠆗󠇒󠄾󠇗󠇜󠄂󠅊󠇣󠅚󠄋︆󠄑󠇝󠅀󠄀󠄙󠆊󠆱󠅛󠆹󠆞󠆷󠆫󠅣󠅤󠄓The real question, the one that unlocks a more profitable and equitable future, is this: How do we prove the value of our work in a world where content travels at the speed of light? 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠄅󠆊󠆋󠅲󠅅󠄜󠄰󠇓󠆃󠄄󠅢󠄠󠄇󠆈󠄡󠅷󠄐︋︌󠆝󠇞︊󠄏󠆠󠄸󠇢󠅧󠇓󠄘󠆗󠅻︈󠅺󠄏󠄐󠄅󠄙󠅒󠄹󠇡To answer that, we have to change the nature of evidence itself. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢︇󠄇󠇧󠆼󠅌󠇐󠄸󠅃󠅿󠆆󠄮󠆏󠆓󠇞󠇃󠇄󠆓󠆗󠄫󠄘󠄾󠄺󠆜︆󠅧󠆣󠄶󠄄󠅽󠆌󠄽󠇂󠅆󠅹󠆔󠆭︋󠄼󠇒󠆬A new future is becoming reality, one that moves the industry from 26% statistical guessing to 100% mathematical proof. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠆘󠄀󠄒󠇓󠇝󠆆󠄶󠄯󠆓󠄎󠆀󠄺︉󠅻󠇣󠅿󠅠󠅻󠆷󠄭󠇒󠅻󠇃󠄘󠄼󠆊󠄂󠅾󠄉︃󠅾󠇒󠆚󠇠󠆊󠄆󠅾󠆠󠅓󠆶As co-chairs of the C2PA's text authentication task force, we are architecting this future, building a new foundation that embeds cryptographic proof of origin directly into content from the moment of its creation. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠆷󠇍󠅌󠆧︁󠆃󠄱󠇉󠆔󠇅󠆰󠅼󠇙󠄘󠆾󠆼󠆶󠅸󠅇󠄟󠄆󠇑󠄑󠅍󠅖󠅪󠄬󠇊󠆋󠄮󠅜󠇊󠇍󠅣󠄲󠅪︈󠅨󠅥󠆲Imagine if every article, every paragraph, every single sentence had its own unique, unforgeable, digital fingerprint that protected publishers and content creators. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠆝󠆤󠄝︋󠄜󠇘󠄳󠅭󠆄󠄲󠄢󠅀󠆴󠄇󠄟︎󠇢󠆆󠅖󠆷󠅐󠄘󠄱󠆵󠄧󠆈󠅽󠇧󠆾󠇅󠆢󠅦󠄶󠅊︊󠇘󠄏󠄛󠇃󠆉This is a forensic-level identifier that travels with your content everywhere it goes, providing mathematical certainty of its journey and use. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠆄󠇋󠅃󠆗︃󠄊󠄾󠇊󠅸󠆳󠇢󠇆︍󠆧󠆗󠅤󠄼󠇔󠇒︎󠆣󠇋󠄴󠅯︅󠆃󠄩󠅻󠇢︉󠇜󠇪󠅈󠅔󠅬󠄼󠅓󠇟󠄃󠅭When every sentence has its own unique fingerprint, the entire dynamic with AI developers transforms. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠆶󠆨󠅐󠅬󠆇󠇃󠄹󠆧󠅽󠆌󠄚󠆠󠄃󠆆󠄕󠇦󠆫󠅾󠇎󠅒󠅻󠄘󠅦󠄓󠇛󠇚󠅵󠅍󠄝󠆇󠅼️󠄩󠄏󠄉󠅯󠅣󠆙󠆋󠆋Suddenly, a high-risk legal gamble can be replaced by a licensing agreement. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠅆󠄀󠅑󠇐󠇁󠇀󠄺︃󠆕󠄃󠆂󠅇︂󠆲󠄵󠆉󠄒󠅳󠆡󠄾󠇇󠆀󠇩󠆷󠆚󠄟󠅇󠆴︉󠅵󠅬󠇇󠄥󠇞󠅚󠄥󠇯󠇓󠄭󠅃The argument over if an AI model used your content is replaced by a data report showing exactly what it used and how it performed. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠆠󠆋󠆃󠅚󠇭󠆭󠄺󠇁󠆬󠅲󠅮󠅵󠄠󠇩󠇀󠅻󠇥󠄽󠆴󠆏󠄟󠄍󠄱󠇠󠆠︊󠆈󠆼󠅻󠄹󠇪󠅪︁󠆈󠅹󠄬󠆎󠄺󠆱󠆛This is the dawn of a new licensing economy. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠇇󠇡󠆳󠄘󠆱󠄽󠄺︀󠆠󠆳󠄤󠄌󠆻󠇏󠅞󠇁󠆑󠅊󠄶󠅱󠆞󠄚󠇁︅︎︃󠄠󠆸󠄕󠅍󠇢󠄿󠇓󠇜󠆯︂󠆼󠄤󠅙󠅴The energy we once spent on defense can now be channeled into building value. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠄱󠆾󠅓󠇆󠄒󠆋󠄻󠆮󠆯󠄕󠇥󠅶󠇋󠄍︇󠄋󠅓󠇒󠇁︀󠇝󠇊󠇀󠆟󠄴󠆟󠄯󠄎󠆱󠄑󠄓󠆎󠄛󠆺󠇔󠆰󠄐󠄳󠄢󠅎The legal department, once a cost center, becomes a powerful engine for a new and predictable revenue stream. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠇠󠅔󠅣󠄌󠄟󠇜󠄼󠅇󠆠󠅜󠇛󠇠󠆏︁󠄲󠄜󠅴󠅤󠄬󠄅󠅂󠄽󠆼󠇄󠅽󠅞󠅖︉󠇥󠄥󠇎󠇙󠅜󠅟󠇑󠆿󠆘󠄢︅︃This is already starting to happen. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢︊󠆌󠇋︃󠇂󠆚󠄾󠄞󠆀󠄣󠇡󠆣󠄪󠇯󠇤󠄗󠆄󠄟󠄅󠅆󠄹︎󠅘󠄎󠇌󠅣󠄯󠄜󠅞󠄅󠄕󠇂󠄜󠆵︈󠄇󠅓󠇬󠄞󠄏The most forward-thinking publishers are moving beyond theory and are actively building the infrastructure for this new economy. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠅞󠄝󠅏󠇏󠅥󠇥󠄸󠆈󠅿󠄥󠆞󠄐󠄪󠆍󠄷󠆙󠄍󠄽󠄲󠄲󠄣󠆐󠆖󠅅󠅹󠇝󠅩󠅛󠄝󠇪󠅳󠇃󠄿󠇒󠅮󠄬󠆨󠄛󠄙󠅪They understand that first-movers will not just participate in this market; they will be the ones who define its terms. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠇞󠆭󠆳󠆿󠇖󠅙󠄲󠅽󠅱󠅄󠅞󠄘󠆹󠆜󠅱󠅰󠅓󠄱󠅼󠇞󠆡󠄬󠆭️󠆼󠅢󠅣󠇃󠇊󠆨󠄚󠅇󠇟󠄉󠆰󠆛󠇟󠆓󠆘󠇟The future of content is built on cryptographic proof, creating the infrastructure for a new licensing economy. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠄺󠅭󠇪󠆿󠆄󠆨󠄻󠇨󠆐󠆻󠄣󠄫󠅕󠅳󠄇󠆻󠇆󠇗󠇒󠅓󠇏󠅒󠆀󠅭󠅓󠅭󠆮󠅕󠆣󠄱󠄣󠄵󠆾󠅀󠅻󠅔󠆛󠇖󠇕󠆬The standards for this transformation are being architected now. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠅱󠅌󠆗︎󠆣󠅅󠄸󠄲󠅻󠇁󠅜󠅯󠆏󠆘󠇅󠇤󠄎󠇚󠄨󠄺󠇜󠆇󠆃󠆃󠄕󠄨󠆭󠄵︌󠄇󠅧󠇍󠇠󠅸󠄭󠄨󠄗󠅿󠆈󠄓If you believe in a future where content value is mathematically provable, let's build it together. 󠇟󠇠󠇡󠇢󠆀󠇪󠇏󠇦󠅞󠄑󠄹󠄐󠅵󠄕󠇎󠅐󠄭󠅏󠇕󠇄󠇭󠆳󠅎󠄷󠆥󠅁󠇁󠅵󠆪󠅒󠇞󠇜󠆞󠆄󠆕󠅴󠇝󠄂󠇁󠅶󠇆󠅺󠅜󠇖The standard is being written, will you help shape it?
